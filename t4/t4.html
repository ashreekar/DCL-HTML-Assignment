Having your Node.js Cake and Eating It Too

This post was written by Rob Tweed, director of M/Gateway Developments Ltd. The company focuses on web and NoSQL database technologies, especially in healthcare.

The QEWD.js framework aims to provide the benefits of Node.js including performance and unified front-end/back-end development, while reducing concerns about concurrency and heavy CPU processing.

"Yes, I like to think you can now have your Node.js cake and eat it too."
- Rob Tweed
